Nextcloud, dressed as a real Mac citizen.

Cirruscope brings your Nextcloud into a fast, native macOS app — real windows, native notifications, and none of the browser-tab clutter.

Everything feels right at home.

Made for macOS

Built entirely in Swift and AppKit, Cirruscope looks, feels, and behaves like it belongs on your Mac.

First class member on your Mac

Lives in your Dock and menu bar and switches with Cmd-Tab — not a browser tab pretending to be an app.

Real Window Management

Open multiple windows, move them between spaces, and use Mission Control and Stage Manager like any native app.

Light and fast

A thin native shell around WebKit means quick launches and a light footprint — no bundled browser engine to drag along.

Full Size Content

Content extends edge-to-edge into the title bar for an immersive, distraction-free view of your files.

Native Sidebar Control

The Nextcloud sidebar behaves like a real macOS sidebar — translucent, collapsible, and consistent with your system.

Speed Dials

Pin the files, folders, and apps you open most to a personal speed dial for one-click access.

Native Notifications

Nextcloud notifications arrive through Notification Center, just like every other Mac app.

Spotlight, Siri & Shortcuts

Jump straight to any Nextcloud app from Spotlight, ask Siri to open it, or add it to your own Shortcuts — your server's apps are available across macOS.

File Transfers

Uploads and downloads use native macOS transfer handling, with progress in your Dock and Downloads folder.

Any Nextcloud Server

Works with any Nextcloud instance — your own self-hosted server or one run by a provider you trust.

See how Cirruscope fits into mass deployment, configuration profiles, and branded builds for organizations.
