Private by design

Your Nextcloud stays yours.

Cirruscope is a thin container: it simply loads your Nextcloud in a native window. Your files and credentials travel directly between your Mac and your server — never through us.

check_circleNo accounts, no cloud, no data collection†
check_circleOpen source & auditable, end to end
check_circleConnects only to servers you choose
Read the privacy policyarrow_forward

† No-collection refers to Cirruscope itself. Your data still lives on — and is processed by — the Nextcloud server you connect to, under its operator's privacy terms.
