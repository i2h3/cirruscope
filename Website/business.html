Cirruscope for Business

We're designing and developing Cirruscope with IT departments, businesses, and enterprises in mind — from mass deployment to managed devices.

01

Mass device management

Deploy Cirruscope across every Mac in your fleet with the MDM you already use — Jamf, Kandji, Mosyle, or Apple Business Manager — and keep every install current automatically.

02

Configuration profiles

Ship a configuration profile that pre-fills the server address, sign-in policy, and default settings, so people open Cirruscope already connected instead of facing a setup screen.

03

Managed devices

Cirruscope will respect the constraints your organization already sets — managed-device restrictions, compliance requirements, and update policies all carry over from macOS.

04

Customized, branded builds

Ship Cirruscope under your own name and icon, pointed at your Nextcloud instance by default — the same native experience, wearing your brand.

Built for App Store distribution.

Deploy through Apple Business Manager

Once Cirruscope reaches the Mac App Store, you can add it in Apple Business Manager and assign it to managed Macs through your MDM — installed silently, kept current by the App Store, with no personal Apple ID required.

Private, branded builds via Custom Apps

Apple's Custom Apps distribute a build privately to your organization alone, off the public store. It's the same channel a branded Cirruscope — your name, your icon, your Nextcloud by default — could reach your teams through.
